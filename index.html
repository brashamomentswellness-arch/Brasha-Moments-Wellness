About Brasha Moments Wellness

Leading the way in integrated mental health and addiction recovery services in Kenya

Our Mission & Vision

At Brasha Moments Wellness, we believe that every individual deserves access to compassionate, evidence-based mental health and addiction treatment. Founded on principles of dignity, respect, and holistic healing, we provide comprehensive psychiatric, psychological, and substance abuse counseling services.

Our approach integrates clinical excellence with genuine care, creating a safe environment where healing begins. We combine modern therapeutic techniques with cultural sensitivity to address the unique needs of each person who walks through our doors.

Our vision is to become Kenya's premier center for mental wellness and addiction recovery, breaking stigmas and building healthier communities one life at a time.

Dr. Okello Odhiambo

Chief Executive Officer

"Our commitment at Brasha Moments Wellness goes beyond treatment—we're dedicated to transforming lives. Every patient receives personalized care tailored to their unique journey toward recovery and mental wellness. Together, we create lasting change."

Board of Directors members will be announced as we continue to grow and serve our community.

Our Comprehensive Services

Evidence-based treatment programs designed for your unique needs

🧠

Psychiatric Services

Comprehensive psychiatric evaluations, diagnosis, and medication management by experienced professionals. We treat depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and other mental health conditions.

💬

Psychological Counseling

Individual, group, and family therapy sessions using evidence-based approaches including CBT, DBT, and trauma-informed care. Safe space for healing and personal growth.

🌱

Drug Addiction Treatment

Specialized programs for drug addiction recovery including detoxification support, rehabilitation, relapse prevention, and long-term recovery planning.

🛡️

Substance Abuse Counseling

Comprehensive substance abuse counseling addressing alcohol, prescription medications, and other substances. Includes individual therapy and support groups.

👥

Group Therapy Programs

Supportive group environments where individuals share experiences, build coping skills, and create lasting connections with others on similar journeys.

🏠

Family Support Services

Family counseling and education programs to help loved ones understand mental health and addiction, improve communication, and support recovery.
